Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Money Beavers collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our service.

2. Information Collection

We collect the following information:

  • Email access for collecting invoice emails
  • Transaction data extracted from your emails
  • Basic account information for service access

3. Data Storage

All your financial data is stored exclusively in your personal Google Sheets. We do not store or maintain copies of your financial information on our servers.

4. Data Usage

We use your information to:

  • Process and extract transaction details from your emails
  • Update your Google Sheets with transaction records
  • Improve our service functionality

5. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal or financial information with third parties. Your data remains private and accessible only through your Google Sheets.

6. Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of data collection

8. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our support page.
