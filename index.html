Frequently Asked Questions

Where is my data stored?

All your data is stored securely in your personal Google Sheets. We don't store or access your financial information on our servers.

Is my data shared with anyone?

No, your data remains private and is only accessible through your Google Sheets. We don't share or sell your information to third parties.

How secure is the connection to my Gmail?

We use Google's secure OAuth authentication to access your Gmail. We only read invoice-related emails and never store your email content.
