Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 1, 2025

At Beavers Money, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our service. Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using Beavers Money, you consent to the data practices described in this statement.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Gmail™ Data

Our service requires access to your Gmail™ messages to identify and extract financial information from receipts and invoices. This access is read-only and limited to the purpose of providing our service.

1.2 Google Sheets™ Access

We require permission to access and modify the specific Google Sheet™(s) that you designate for storing your financial data extracted from emails.

1.3 Usage Data

We collect information on how you interact with our service, including access times, pages viewed, and the features you use. This helps us improve our service and troubleshoot issues.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for various purposes, including:

  • To provide and maintain our service
  • To identify and extract financial data from your emails
  • To populate your Google Sheet™ with the extracted data
  • To notify you about changes to our service
  • To provide customer support
  • To gather analysis or valuable information to improve our service
  • To detect, prevent, and address technical issues

3. Data Storage

3.1 Your Financial Data

Your financial data extracted from emails is stored exclusively in your own Google Sheets™. We do not store copies of this data on our servers. The processing of your email content to extract financial information happens temporarily during the extraction process, and we do not retain copies of your emails or their content.

4. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@beavers.money.
